## Overview

Your phone now has **TWO layers of defense** against unauthorized access:

### 🤫 Layer 1: SILENT ALERT (Stealth Mode)

## 🚀 Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Enable Silent Alert Mode (Layer 1)

1. Open **Axiom Security** app
2. Go to **Settings** ⚙️
3. Tap **🔒 Voice Codeword Lock**
4. Toggle **"🤫 Silent Alert Mode"** → **ON** (Green)
5. Set your **trusted contact** in main settings (for WhatsApp alerts)

**What it does:**
- ✅ Monitors device unlock events
- ✅ Detects if unlock is authorized (recent biometric auth)
- ✅ If unauthorized → Silently captures 4-5 photos
- ✅ Gets GPS location
- ✅ Sends WhatsApp alert to you
- ✅ Person has NO IDEA this happened

### Step 2: Enable Voice Lock (Layer 2)

1. Same screen → Toggle **"🔒 Voice Lock Protection"** → **ON**
2. Grant **"Usage Access"** permission
3. Set your **voice codeword**: "axiom unlock" (or custom phrase)
4. Select which apps to protect (Google Pay, etc.)

**What it does:**
- ✅ Monitors when protected apps are opened
- ✅ Locks device with red screen
- ✅ Only your voice unlocks
- ✅ Takes more photos after 3 failures

## 🎭 Why TWO Layers?

### Layer 1 (Silent) - EVIDENCE GATHERING
**Purpose:** Collect evidence WITHOUT alerting the intruder

**Advantages:**
- ✅ They don't know they're being monitored
- ✅ They won't hide their face
- ✅ They won't turn off the phone
- ✅ They won't disable GPS
- ✅ You get clear photos and location
- ✅ Works even if they never open protected apps

**Triggers:**
- Device unlocked without recent biometric auth (within 10 mins)
- 2+ failed biometric/PIN attempts

### Layer 2 (Voice Lock) - ACTIVE PROTECTION
**Purpose:** Prevent access to sensitive data

**Advantages:**
- ✅ Blocks access to banking/payment apps
- ✅ Blocks access to messages/emails
- ✅ Cannot be bypassed
- ✅ Gives you remote time to act
- ✅ Takes MORE evidence if they persist

**Triggers:**
- Protected app (Google Pay, WhatsApp, etc.) opened
- User not authorized (no auth in last 5 mins)

## 🔧 Technical Details

### Silent Monitor Service

**File:** `SilentIntruderMonitorService.kt`

**How it detects unauthorized access:**
```kotlin
1. Monitors screen unlock events (ACTION_USER_PRESENT)
2. Checks: Last biometric auth time
3. If auth > 10 minutes ago → Unauthorized
4. Triggers silent alert
```

**Authorization tracking:**
```kotlin
- Biometric auth success → Save timestamp
- Authorization valid for: 10 minutes
- After 10 mins → Silent alert triggers on unlock
```

**Photo capture:**
```kotlin
- Uses front camera
- Captures 5 photos with 500ms delay between each
- Saves to: /data/data/com.example.axiom/files/intruders/
- Sends first 3 photos via WhatsApp
```

### Voice Lock Service

**File:** `UnauthorizedAccessProtectionService.kt`

**How it detects app access:**
```kotlin
1. Monitors UsageStatsManager (every 1 second)
2. Checks: Which app came to foreground
3. If app in protected list → Check authorization
4. If unauthorized → Launch EmergencyLockActivity
```

**Authorization window:**
```kotlin
- Authorization valid for: 5 minutes
- Shorter than silent monitor (10 mins)
- Ensures double-check security
```

## 🛠️ Customization

### Change Authorization Timeouts

**Silent Monitor (10 mins):**
```kotlin
File: SilentIntruderMonitorService.kt
Line: private const val AUTH_VALIDITY_DURATION = 10 * 60 * 1000L

Change to: 5 * 60 * 1000L (5 minutes)
Or: 30 * 60 * 1000L (30 minutes)
```

**Voice Lock (5 mins):**
```kotlin
File: UnauthorizedAccessProtectionService.kt  
Line: val authValidDuration = 5 * 60 * 1000L

Change to: 10 * 60 * 1000L (10 minutes)
Or: 2 * 60 * 1000L (2 minutes - very strict)
```

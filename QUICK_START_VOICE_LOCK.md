# 🔒 Voice Codeword Lock - Quick Start Guide

## What This Feature Does

**Problem Solved:** Someone picks up your phone and tries to open Google Pay, WhatsApp, or banking apps.

**Solution:** Device **instantly locks** with a full-screen red warning. Only YOUR voice speaking YOUR secret codeword can unlock it.

---

## 🚀 Quick Setup (2 Minutes)

### Step 1: Enable Protection
1. Open **Axiom Security** app
2. Tap **Settings** ⚙️ (top right)
3. Tap **🔒 Voice Codeword Lock** (the blue card)
4. Toggle **"Protection Active"** to **ON** 🟢
5. When system dialog appears → Tap **"Enable"**
6. Find **"Axiom"** in the list → Toggle **ON**

### Step 2: Set Your Secret Codeword
1. Tap **"Current Codeword"** card
2. Delete the default text
3. Type your secret phrase: Examples:
   - ✅ `"axiom unlock me now"`
   - ✅ `"my secret garden"`
   - ✅ `"blue sky sunshine"`
   - ✅ `"open sesame please"`
4. Tap **"Save"** ✓

### Step 3: Test It!
1. Tap **"Test Lock Screen"** button
2. 🔴 Red lock screen appears
3. Tap the **microphone button** 🎤
4. **Say your codeword** clearly
5. ✅ Screen unlocks = Success!

**You're protected!** 🎉

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Choosing a Good Codeword
✅ **DO:**
- Use 2-4 words
- Use common words (better recognition)
- Make it memorable
- Examples: `"my dog rex"`, `"pizza time now"`

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use 1 word only (too short)
- Use complex/rare words (hard to recognize)
- Use names (easy to guess)

### Best Practices
1. **Test in quiet place first** - Make sure voice recognition works
2. **Speak clearly** - Not too fast, not too slow
3. **Remember your phrase** - Write it down safely
4. **Tell trusted family** - In case of emergency
5. **Change if compromised** - Easy to update anytime

## 🆘 Emergency Situations

### "I Forgot My Codeword!"
**Solution:**
1. Try common phrases you use
2. After 3 failures → Alert sent to your trusted contact
3. They can help you remember
4. Worst case: Restart phone → Disable protection → Set new codeword

### "Voice Not Working!"
**Quick Fixes:**
1. Speak **louder and clearer**
2. Reduce background noise
3. Try in quiet room
4. Check microphone permission
5. Restart app if needed

### "Someone Keeps Trying!"
**Good News:**
1. After 3 failed attempts:
   - 📸 Photo of intruder taken
   - 🚨 Alert sent to your trusted contact
   - 🔒 Device stays locked
2. Check logs in Dashboard later
3. Contact police if serious threat
